About Our Library

The Parliamentary Service of Ghana Library has been serving the nation's democratic process for over six decades, providing essential information services to support legislative work and public understanding of parliamentary affairs.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, accessible, and reliable information services that support Ghana's parliamentary democracy, enhance legislative effectiveness, and promote public understanding of parliamentary processes and governance.

Our Vision

To be the premier parliamentary information center in West Africa, recognized for excellence in research services, digital innovation, and our contribution to strengthening democratic governance through information accessibility.
