Library Policies

Guidelines for using the Parliamentary Service Library

Membership Policy

Eligibility

  • Members of Parliament and staff
  • Government officials
  • Researchers (by appointment)
  • Registered public members

Borrowing Privileges

Loan Periods

  • MPs: 10 items, 30 days
  • Staff: 5 items, 14 days
  • Public: 2 items, 7 days

Code of Conduct

  • Respect others
  • Maintain quiet study areas
  • No food in the library
  • Follow staff instructions
