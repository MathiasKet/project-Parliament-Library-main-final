Library Services

A comprehensive range of services supports parliamentary work, academic research, and public access to Ghana's legislative information.

Services

  • Conducive study spaces.
  • Current Awareness Services.
  • Customized contributions of individual Members of Parliament.
  • Green Library.
  • Interlibrary loans.
  • Library Research.
  • Reference and Information Service.
  • Reproduction of library resources from the library's collection.
  • Selective Dissemination of Information.

Specialized Services

For Members of Parliament
Exclusive services designed for parliamentary members
  • Priority research requests
  • Dedicated liaison librarian
  • Private study rooms
  • 24/7 digital access
  • Document delivery to offices
  • Legislative tracking alerts
For Academic Institutions
Collaborative services for universities and research centers
  • Inter-library loan programs
  • Student research support
  • Faculty collaboration opportunities
  • Academic conference partnerships
For Media & Journalists
Specialized support for media professionals
  • Press release archives
  • Background briefing materials
  • Fact-checking support
  • Historical context research
  • Media kit preparation
  • Expedited document requests

Access Requirements

  • Valid National ID required for in-person visits
  • Registration needed for digital services
  • Special clearance required for restricted documents
  • Institutional agreements for bulk access

Usage Guidelines

  • Proper citation required for all materials
  • Commercial use restrictions apply
  • Copyright laws must be respected
  • Fair use policy for downloads and printing
